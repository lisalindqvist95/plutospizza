Meet Pluto

Hello, I’m Pluto! I'm a full-time photographer and food enthusiast. My favourite food since I was a kid has always been pizza, what can I say, I’m half Italian so it’s in my blood. I started this blog as a way for me to rate the pizzas I eat, usually in my local area which is in Vasastan (Stockholm, Sweden). This will be beneficial for me to keep track of my favourites but it might also interest some of you so I decided to share my journey here. I hope that you will want to come and explore the wonderful world of pizzas with me. Best, Pluto
